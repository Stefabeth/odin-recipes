Bolos de Coco

Description

Bolos de Coco translate to coconut cakes. If you're a fan of coconut, you're in for a treat. Just be careful because it can definitely be hard to stop at just one! You can find these yummy little pastries in caf√©s all over Portugal any time of the year. They are also simple to make and easy to carry, so consider making these for your next party or potluck.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cup shredded coconut
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line 3 muffin cups with paper liners.
  2. Mix coconut, sugar, eggs, and lemon zest together in a large bowl. Scoop coconut mixture into lined muffin cups.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until firm and golden, 15 to 20 minutes.
