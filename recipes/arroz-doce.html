Arroz Doce

Description

Rice pudding is a recognized dessert in many parts of the world, but in Portugal, it is called Arroz Doce, which translates to Sweet Rice. Not only is Arroz Doce great tasting, but it is beautiful to look at, often decorated with cinnamon patterns. Arroz Doce can be enjoyed all year round, but it is traditionally served on Christmas Eve. When dining in Portugal, you can try asking for "Um arroz doce, por favor" ("One arroz doce, please"), and they will likely have it. If you don't get a chance to order this in Portugal, no worries! Read on to learn how easy it is to make this delicious dish.
